I am pleased to announce that we have been awarded a new T32 training grant from the NIH/NIMHD titled **"Increasing and Diversifying Future AI-Precision Nutrition Research Workforce."**

This program is a partnership between **Prairie View A&M University (PVAMU)** and the **Children’s Nutritional Research Center at Baylor College of Medicine**. Our goal is to train Ph.D. students and post-doctoral fellows in precision nutrition research, equipping them with advanced AI and machine learning skills.

As a Historically Black College and University (HBCU), PVAMU is uniquely positioned to significantly improve diversity in the future workforce of precision nutritional research.

* **Role:** Contact PI
* **Funding Agency:** NIH/NIMHD
* **Period:** 09/23/2023 – 04/30/2028
